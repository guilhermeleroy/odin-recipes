Macarronada - Pasta

Ingredients

  • 300 g of pasta
  • 1/2 tomate sauce can
  • 1 sliced in small pieces onion
  • 2 sliced sausages
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • salt and pepper
  • parmesan cheese

Preparations

  1. Boil the water. When it boils, add some salt to cook the pasta
  2. Cook the pasta as it is indicated in the packaging and reserve it
  3. Use the cooking pot to cook the onion with the butter. As it turns gold, put in the sausages
  4. Add the tomato sauce. Adjust salt and pepper
  5. When the sauce is well cooked and seasoned, add the pasta. It's ready to serve. Parmesan cheese cn be put on the plate as you wish
