Brigadeiro

Ingredients

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of powder chocolate

Preparations

  1. Mix all of the ingredients in a cooking pot
  2. In low heat, mix the ingredients until you can see the bottom of the pot
  3. Turn off the heat. Wait for it to cool of. Make ball shapes with the mixture
  4. If you want, you can cover it with chocolate dust or granulated chocolate
