Mojito

Ingredients

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 lime, cut into 4 wedges
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 1 cup of ice cubes
  • 1/2 cup of club soda
  • 50 mL of white rum

Preparations

  1. Place mint leaves and 1 lime wedge into a sturdy glass.
  2. Use a muddler to crush the mint and lime to release the mint oils and lime juice
  3. Add 2 more lime wedges and the sugar, and muddle again to release the lime juice. Do not strain the mixture
  4. Fill the glass almost to the top with ice
  5. Pour the rum over the ice, and fill the glass with carbonated water. Stir, taste, and add more sugar if desired
  6. Garnish with the remaining lime wedge
